Math Guide

The site is relatively straightforward to use. Put your left equation on the left, right equation on the right. Multiplication signs should be typed as * (e.g. 6 multiplied by 2 would be 6*2). Division should be handled as / (e.g. 4 divided by 2 would be 4/2) Addition and Subtraction are handled as normal.

Click Submit once you're done and your equations should appear on the scale. The scale should move based on which equation has a higher value, and the status bar should tell you if the equations are equal or not.
